Type of AI

Reactive AI

Reactive AI responds only to what is happening right now. It does not store memories or learn from past interactions. Each response is based purely on the current input, making it simple but limited in ability.

Limited Memory AI

Limited memory AI can use past data to improve its responses or decisions. It analyzes patterns over time, such as user behavior or previous inputs, but does not have deep understanding or long-term awareness.

Theory of Mind AI

Theory of mind AI is designed to understand human emotions, intentions, and social behavior. Although still mostly theoretical, it aims to interact more naturally by recognizing how people feel and respond accordingly.

Self-Aware AI

Self-aware AI would have consciousness and awareness of its own existence. This type of AI exists only in theory and fiction, as current technology has not reached this level of intelligence.

Narrow AI

Narrow AI is built to perform a specific task efficiently. It cannot go beyond its programmed function, but within that area, it can be highly accurate and reliable.

General AI (AGI)

General AI refers to a system that can perform any intellectual task a human can do. It would be flexible, adaptive, and capable of learning across different fields, but it has not yet been achieved.

Generative AI

Generative AI creates new content such as text, images, or music. It uses patterns learned from data to produce original outputs, making it useful for creative and problem-solving tasks.
